INSTRUCTIONS

Customers will arrive at the front of the restaurant. Click on them, then click on an empty table to seat them. When a customer is ready to order, they'll raise their flipper. Click on them and they'll give their order. The kitchen will begin to prepare their food and once it's ready, the dish will appear on the counter! Click on the dish and bring it to the customer. Don't take too long, or the customer will get impatient and leave.

Once a customer finishes eating, they'll leave their table. Click on their table to clean it up and claim your tip!

The restaurant closes at 9pm, so keep an eye on the clock and serve customers as quickly as you can! The yellow number on the cash register how much money you need to make by the end of the shift.

At the end of the day, you can use your leftover tips to buy upgrades for the restaurant! These upgrades can help you move faster, help customers stay patient, and increase the amount of money you can earn from tips.

At the end of each day, you'll earn a star. You can use your stars to buy new outfits or customize your restaurant with new themes.

HINT: When you see a pair of lovebirds, seat them at a table with flowers!
